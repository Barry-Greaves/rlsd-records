Rustal

Peter Sweeney aka Rustal is an artist who demonstrates a truly admirable thirst for artistic development. Though he already has compiled a significant discography, Rustal's intrinsic dedication to music has seen him continue to explore the electronic music world, and expand his talents as both a Producer, DJ and Live Performer .

Releasing music since 2008 across a myriad of labels with a refreshing blend of different techno, the Dublin native has played in Detroit, Tresor and alongside some of the biggest names in the game such as Surgeon , Luke Slater , Dj Bone , 999999999 and Function .
