Gianmarco Silvetti - Don't Forget The Groove


RLSD Podcast 027

RLSD Records are proud to present the next guest on our podcast series, Gianmarco Silvetti.

The Italian Dj, producer and label owner has put together a killer mix full of energy and tight grooves. The Berlin resident has played in clubs such as Tresor, Suicide Circus and Ispe. Gianmarco also runs the label Secret Keywords which has been operating since 2014 and has released a string of excellent releases.

We hope you enjoy this as much as we do !
