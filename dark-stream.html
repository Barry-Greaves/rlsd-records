RLSD DARK STREAM

Dark Stream Vol 1 - Barry Greaves

For the inagaugral edition of the RLSD Dark Stream, resident Barry Greaves steps up with an session of dark and heavy techno.

The mix moves from the clipped and rugged uk sounds of Blawan and Rene wise into dark acidic teritory courtesy of techno legend Luke Slater. Things then take a heavier turn with the industrial but funky sounds of Surgeon, Takaaki Itoh a Fran Hartnett.

Dark Stream Vol 2 - Barry Greaves

RLSD resident Barry Greaves takes the reigns again for the second edition of the Dark Stream.

On this outing we a treated a mix up of funked up techno full of tribal loops, wiggling acid lines and massive chords.

Dark Stream Vol 3 - Rustal

RLSD welcome back Peter Sweeney AKA Rustal for the 3rd edition of the Dark Stream.

Fresh off the release of his Overshadow EP on Gynoid Audio, Rustal delivers 30 minutes of machine funk for the Dark Stream.

Dark Stream Vol 4 - Casper Hastings

RLSD Records welcome Casper Hastings for the 4th edition of the Dark Stream.

Casper featured on our Tensional Ground VA and has just put out a killer new EP on Sticky Ground. No slouch on the decks either, Casper skillfully weaves together an hour of banging and leftfield techno for the Dark Stream.

Dark Stream Vol 5 - Jon Hussey

RLSD Records are delighted to welcome back Jon Hussey for the 5th edition of the Dark Stream. Having previously released on RLSD Records Jon has gone on to put out killer material on Earwiggle and most recently featured on CeiliCollective with the excellent "Fembot Motion".

Jon has provided us with a very special mix exploring the sounds of Planet Dub where he cut his teeth as a dj. Here are a few words from the man himself on the set :

“The idea behind the Livestream mix came to me during the lockdown while I was going through old archive records, listening and reflecting back on the Techno that I began playing as a DJ. I gathered together about 50 cherished records that I played in the first Techno club night I DJ’ed at and helped co run called ‘Planet Dub’ and I thought about putting together a mix to capture the essence of the amazing music from that time, some of which laid the blueprints for what was the come in the future of Techno”

Dark Stream Vol 6 - Offtrack

RLSD welcome Offtrack for the 6th edition of the Dark Stream this Friday.

One of Irelands brightest young production talents and a quality DJ, Offtrack has provided us with an hour of looping and banging techno skillfully mixed up on 3 decks.

Dark Stream Vol 7 - TR-One

RLSD Records are deligthed to welcome TR One for the 7th edition of the Dark Stream.

A prolific Irish producer who needs little introduction at this point , Tr One has been releasing quality Detroit influenced house and techno on labels like, Dont be afraid, 393 Records, Lunar Disko & Apartment Records for many years.

A dedicated and diverse dj , Tr One has provided us with a killer mix, weaving his way through dub techno, electro, house and Detroit techno in some style.

Dark Stream Vol 8 - Gamadon

RLSD Records welcome Gamadon for the 8th edition on the Dark Stream. Honing his skills since the late 90s Gamadon has built up a reputation as a true master of the turntables, having played everywhere from Mullingar to Singapore along the way.

Most recently Gamadon has set up the Warhouse Manifesto label as an outlet for his never ending stream of quality mixes and as a platform to release his own productions on vinyl.

Gamadon has provided us with an hour and a half of absolutely slamming techno mixed to a tee.

Dark Stream Vol 9 - Lee Holman

RLSD Records welcome back Lee Holman for the 9th edition of the Dark Stream.

A firm favourtie in RLSD HQ, Lee has been releasing proper techno records on some of the best labels around from Ferox to his own Kawl imprint for many years. Lee also featured on RLSD 001, where he delivered an incendiary remix of Doug Cooney's Bias.

Performing in clubs since the late nineties, Lee has played throughout Europe, North America, South America, Asia as a dj and live act. It's easy to see why here as Lee's turntable skills are in full display in this 3 deck masterclass.

Dark Stream Vol 10 - Doug Cooney

RLSD Dark Stream returns tonight with a special mix from our own Doug Cooney.

Since featuring on RLSD 001 Doug has released a string of quality techno EPs on labels like Bastardo Electrico, GND Records, Gynoid Audio and Orbis records which have gained worldwide support and have led to some big bookings around Europe.

An avid vinyl collector for many years Doug has mastered the art of playing records. His skills are in full display here as he weaves together an hour of killer techno for the Dark Stream.

Dark Stream Vol 11 - Jay Galligan & Dual

RLSD Dark Stream is back for the 11th edition with a very special AV mix from Jay Galligan and visual artist Dual_000 Jay Galligan is a name that will be very familiar to anyone who going to techno gigs in Dublin in the 2000s, holding down a long term residency at the legendary Elecric City and also running his own night Test which would regularly host the best in Irish and international electronic artists. We have also worked with Jay in the past on the BTC events which saw Robert Hood, James Ruskin, Claro Intelecto & Delta Funktionnen play. A brilliant dj to boot, Jay has a deep understanding of electronic music which clearly shines through in this mix. Here are a few words from the man himself:

"The concept for this mix came from collating the past 6 months of tunes I had collected for the Room 237 radio show I have been hosting with Marcus 237 aka Black Amiga. I had taken a break from listening to a lot of electronic music. It wasn’t planned but the return to it was spurred on by Marcus giving me a kick up the arse by giving me slots at his night. Fair props to him for getting the boot out. The break from tunes was probably about 2 years in duration and a lot had changed in electronic music in that time. I had to get reconnected with ‘what was what’ and rediscover what I liked when reviewing all the new music that had emerged. It was super enjoyable and I suppose this is where I found the inertia to curate / programme the radio show with Black Amiga.

The video mix was recorded after a meal in Marcus and Ursula’s house. Completed in one take. Ursula aka Dual_000 is an incredible visual artist and videographer specialising in video art, music promos and live AV. Working with filmed footage captured from a variety of sourced content, she experiments with CCTV, VHS and CRT monitors and analogue hardware to create a visual collage of imagery combined with granular textures, effects and errors. Her ability to edit live analogue visuals is astounding and we really wanted to add a different perspective to the video DJ set format" Check out some of Dual_000’s work here – https://vimeo.com/dual https://www.instagram.com/catwarrior_000/

If you like the tunes selected for this mix, you can find all the previous Room 237 Radio show on my mixcloud https://www.mixcloud.com/jaygalligan Or join us live every 4 th Wednesday of the month on 1020 radio http://1020.live/
