James Begley - Form & Function


RLSD Podcast 023

Drogheda based techno DJ and producer has quietly remained behind the scenes for many years spending the last decade developing his prowess as a producer and the last 2 years perfecting his abilities on the decks.

Known to few, his unreleased tracks have covertly featured in clubs and on digital radio platforms both in Ireland and abroad, He has also started to make ground with his DJing as of recent with a well received appearance on the much respected RLSD Records podcast series, James was also due to play in the Button Factory main room on St. Patricks Day this year and also at 39/40 as support for Neit Fleit & Mylo Spykers but due to club closures his time behind the decks has been put on hold once again.

With both his own tracks and DJ sets he tries to maintain a progression in both energy and tension with underlying intention to give a provoking soundtrack giving homage to those before him but also to put forward his own offering to techno.
