Casper Hastings

Casper Hastings is an electronic producer and DJ from Ireland who has released on labels such as TXTRL, Sticky Ground, RLSD Records, Rave Selekts, Obscuur Records to name a few.

Coming from a small town in south rural Ireland that thrived on the sounds of early techno, Casper began to craft his DJ skills in his early teens around 2006 when he began playing at illegal raves and parties. Since then he has defined his own style and worked his way around the Irish rave scene, building a name for himself.

As well as mastering the skill of DJing from a young age, Casper studied sound engineering in his early twenties and began producing his own music. His productions have a defined, dark sound and often nod to the familiar sounds of old school electro and jungle that influenced Casper from a young age.

Since releasing his own music, Casper has played various locations and clubs around Europe, such as Glasgow, Berlin, Paris, London and Munich
