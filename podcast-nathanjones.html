Nathan Jones- Freq Waves


RLSD Podcast 026

Whether it is building atmosphere and suspense with selections that are hard and intense or moments of melody that grab you.

Each one of Nathan Jones' sets are bound together by powerful rhythms, improvised and entwined with the energy of the dance floor. Sharing energy on a journey as one.

Freedom of expression on a dance floor without boundaries is important to Nathan and he continues to absorb new experiences as a club goer himself, always in search of inspiration and improving his skill as a DJ.

Since crafting his sound for over a decade and holding down residences throughout the club circuit of his hometown, Dublin. His growing tour schedule has taken him to some of Europe’s most prolific club nights, in cities such as Zagreb, Nantes and Berlin.

His discography consists of two solo vinyl EPs on Vision Collector Records, a two track EP on Argentina’s: Concepto Hypnotico, a vinyl release on Delinquent Delivery Records as well as an upcoming 12” on OGMA records.
