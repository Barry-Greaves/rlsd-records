RLSD PODCAST

Our podcast series represents the artists we at RLSD Records connect with, from home and abroad. The series embodies their musical variety and our far reaching tastes.

We choose each selector carefully and in doing so have curated a unique and varied collection of electronic music mixed with passion and skill.

Browse the full collection below and enjoy the unique journey each of our guests will bring on.
