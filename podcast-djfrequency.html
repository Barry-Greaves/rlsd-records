DJ Frequency - Dystopian Nightmare


RLSD Podcast 034

RLSD Records gratefully welcomes New - Old record enthusiast DJ Frequency to our Podcast series. Having recently planted his roots in the Techno Mecca that is Berlin in a bid to Ferment an already quality record collection, DJ Frequency is set to take things up a level.

RLSD Records founding member Doug Cooney formed a strong connection with DJ Frequency when their paths collided in the DIT DJ Soc. Doug had the honour of working alongside DJ Frequency when they embarked on the journey to conquer the competitive national DJ Comp. DJ Frequency who competed at the time as... ?!?! won the competition with ease and has since capitalised on the momentum gained from this conquest.

The RLSD Team are always looking for opportunities to cultivate integrity seeding the future of the Techno Sound and without question DJ Frequency carries that Battle standard. This funked up and fast paced exploration of the genre left us refreshed and excited that the future of Irish Techno is in good hands.
