Shannen Blessing - Mindful


RLSD Podcast 033

Shannen Blessing is a rising & talented DJ and producer based in Ireland. Since attending the Bray Institute of Further Education's DJ Techniques course, under the mentorship of Sunil Sharpe & Doug Cooney, Shannen's adept focus and passion was apparent.

Shannen's ability and confidence in her sound and style is gaining momentum at a prolific and encouraging rate. This mix is testament to her unique and mature ear as Shannen delivers a slick and intricate mix, proving her calibre in the Techno arena.

Shannen has quickly turned her hand to production and applied the same tenacity and integrity making quick progress with the discipline, so, we're looking forward to her first release in the near future. Finally, thank you Shannen, you've delivered a quality mix far beyond your years, Massive RSPKT form the RLSD Crew and we look forward to your exciting future in music.
