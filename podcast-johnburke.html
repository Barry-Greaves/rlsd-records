John Burke - Dejected DATA


RLSD Podcast 025

John Burke steps up in a big way for RLSD proving his calibre in the techno arena.

John recently completed the DJ Course in BIFE under the mentorship of Sunil Sharpe, Defekt & Doug Cooney. Since then John has continued to study Music Production in BIFE after a promising fist year demonstrating a natural flare for DJ Techniques & Music Production.

John placed second in the final of the annual BIFE DJ Battle with over 20 competitors & fierce competition. John was a stand out performer for some as he was the only Person to compete playing solely Vinyl after only 6 months of mixing tuition.

John has since been recruited by the Real Sound Music School as a teaching assistant working closely with Doug Cooney & Darren Devery. John's aptitude in the classroom was apparent from the outset showcasing his communication & technical skills, making him a perfect fit.

John is currently pushing his production and sound at an exponential rate creating an exciting and expansive future for himself in the music business through tenacity, grit and an unquenchable passion for the art form.

Every once in a while talent like John emerges and we at RLSD have him earmarked for our imprint watching his production skill flourish.
